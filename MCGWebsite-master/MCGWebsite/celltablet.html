If you own a smartphone, you probably depend on it daily. We understand that if it gets damaged or stops working correctly, you will need fast smartphone repair service. Our technicians are trained to fix a vast range of smartphone models so that your dependable device can be back in your hands quickly.

No matter what smartphone or tablet you carry, our technicians are trained to diagnose and repair the issues you are experiencing.

General FAQs

Can your technicians only repair cell phones or can they repair other electronic devices as well?

We repair cell phones on a daily basis. In addition, each location is fully able to handle all kinds of electronic repairs including laptops, tablets & gaming systems. We specialize in: iPad repair - iPod repair - Xbox repair - Playstation repair - Wii repair - iPhone repair - Samsung repair - HTC repair - Motorola repair- ZTE repair- laptop repair - Blackberry repair & so much more!

My kid dropped my phone in the toilet, what do I do?

Remove the battery from the phone immediately. There is very high success rate in being able to restore electronic devices that have suffered water damage. A water damaged phone or other tech should be brought in as soon as possible after the damage occurs. Bring your liquid damaged item to us as soon as possible and we will gladly take over from there!

What is going to happen if I no longer wish to have my electronics repair done?

If you make the decision that you no longer want to have your electronics repair work done by us, you may be charged a fee (please ask your sales rep for details). This fee can be waived if you decide to donate your electronic device to our recycling program.

What does the repair price estimate include? Does it include just the labor?

The repair price estimate we give you includes parts and labor.

Can you backup data on my device?

Yes! If you want to backup data stored on your device, you have come to the right place! Our technicians are fully capable of backing up data to ensure you dont lose any of it.

Will my repair cause data loss on my device?

Sometimes certain damage your electronic device receives may cause data loss, but nothing that our technicians do will cause loss of data.

If I currently have a warranty protection on my device, will any repairs you do make my warranty void?

When your item becomes damaged, your warranty protection becomes void.
